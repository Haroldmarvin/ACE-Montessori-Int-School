Our Prepared Environments

We offer four distinct levels of learning, each tailored to the specific developmental "sensitive periods" of your child's growth.

CRECHE

6m - 2yrs

Focusing on sensory-motor development and providing a secure emotional foundation.

NURSERY

2yrs - 4yrs

Building practical life coordination, Grace & Courtesy, and language enrichment.

KINDY

4yrs - 6yrs

Transitioning to mathematical minds and advanced phonics-based reading.

PRIMARY

6yrs - 11yrs

Abstract thinking, research projects, and complex cosmic education studies.

The Five Pillars of ACE

Practical Life

Activities that teach the child to care for themselves and their environment, building focus and order.

Sensorial

Developing and refining the five senses to help children classify the world around them.

Mathematics

Using concrete Montessori materials to understand quantities and the decimal system.

Language & Literacy

A phonetics-rich environment where children learn to write before they read through sand-paper letters.

Culture & Science

Integrating Geography, History, Botany, and Zoology to show the interconnectedness of all life.
